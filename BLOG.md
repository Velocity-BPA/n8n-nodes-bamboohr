# Automate Your HR Workflows: Introducing the BambooHR Node for n8n

We're excited to announce the release of our latest community node for n8n: the BambooHR integration! Developed by Velocity BPA, this node brings powerful HR automation capabilities directly into your n8n workflows.

## The HR Automation Challenge

Human Resources teams juggle countless tasks daily: onboarding new employees, tracking time off requests, managing benefits enrollment, monitoring performance goals, and reviewing time tracking data. These processes often involve manual data entry across multiple systems, leading to errors, delays, and frustrated team members.

Many organizations use BambooHR as their central HR platform but struggle to connect it with other business tools. Whether you need to sync employee data to your payroll system, send Slack notifications for time-off approvals, or create automated onboarding workflows, the lack of seamless integration creates bottlenecks.

## Introducing n8n-nodes-bamboohr

Our new BambooHR node for n8n eliminates these friction points by enabling you to automate virtually any HR process. Connect BambooHR with over 400+ other applications in n8n's ecosystem to create powerful, custom workflows that save time and reduce errors.

## Key Features

The n8n-nodes-bamboohr node provides comprehensive access to BambooHR's functionality:

- **Employee Management**: Retrieve, update, and manage employee records automatically
- **Time Off Tracking**: Automate PTO requests, approvals, and balance updates
- **Benefits Administration**: Streamline benefits enrollment and management
- **Goals & Performance**: Track and update employee goals programmatically
- **Time Tracking**: Access and process time tracking data for payroll and reporting

## Real-World Use Cases

Imagine automatically creating accounts in all your systems when a new employee joins, sending welcome emails, and assigning onboarding tasks—all triggered from BambooHR. Or consider automated time-off notifications that update team calendars and notify project managers instantly.

## Getting Started

Installing the BambooHR node is simple. In your n8n instance, run:


npm install n8n-nodes-bamboohr


Then restart n8n, and you'll find the BambooHR node available in your node palette. Check out the full documentation and source code on our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/Velocity-BPA/n8n-nodes-bamboohr).
